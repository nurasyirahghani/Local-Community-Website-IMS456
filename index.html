A Glimpse:The Presence of Wonder




Kg Layang-Layangan is one of 27 vilagges exist in Federal Territory of Labuan.

The named of the village come from the small island located in front of the village that

was around 80 square meters which used to have many types of snakes and birds named 'Layang-Layang' .

The village is strategically located and gifted with a beautiful and mesmerizing view of the ocean.

The villagers were from a variety of ethnic and races which consist of Kedayan, Brunei,

Kadazan, Cina, and India but the majority of the people were from Kedayan and Brunei.



BRIEF HISTORY


Kg Layang-Layangan not only popular due to its mesmerizing

ocean view, but it is also known for the historical attractions among the tourist.

There is park called Peace Park that serves as a World War II memorial

and poignant reminder of the horrors of war. It is been stated in history that Labuan was

under Japanese occupation for four year before being yielded to Allied Forces on 10th September 1945.

This park was built by the Japanese as a symbol of peace, harmony, and friendship

as well as a tribute to those who lost their lives during the World War II.
